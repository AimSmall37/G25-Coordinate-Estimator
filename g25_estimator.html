G25 Coordinate Estimator

Approximate PCA coordinates from known inter-sample distances via direct solve or weighted multilateration
Two Methods: With 26+ matched references, a Direct Linear Algebra Solver can compute exact coordinates by expanding the Euclidean distance equations and cancelling squared unknowns via row reduction, yielding a closed-form solution. Alternatively, the Multilateration method uses inverse-distance-weighted centroid + gradient descent refinement, and works with any number of references (3+). Use Both mode to compare the two approaches side by side.
